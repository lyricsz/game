About - Lyricsz

Hello once again... This is Lyricsz About Page. And we're here to know more about who they are...

Lyricsz is created by a small group of 3 in 2021. Back in the days, it was created for one main service... To serve the world with Song Lyrics. Lot of development has happened over the years... They (The Lyricsz Team) shift from just text to Lyric Videos. However, this isn't what most of us expect...

This is a Game Developers website not Lyricist. A nanosecond...

In 2023, Lyricsz published apps that relate to song lyrics. And officially shift some of their focuses to creating apps, but met disappointment due to Copyright Issues. Recently as of late 2023 and early 2024, the team has changed their main focus to both Games and Song lyric videos. And that's what gave birth to what you're viewing right now...
